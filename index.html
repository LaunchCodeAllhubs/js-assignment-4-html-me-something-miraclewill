Berries and Shrooms: A modern cocktail from Your Favorite Bartender

A sweet, light, and refreshing drink

This Berries and Shrooms drink is the perfect cocktail for those that love fruity flavors and aromas. It is naturally sweetened and made with fresh strawberries and blueberries. This is the ideal summer cocktail and the prettier, the better!

Equipment Needed

  • Boston Shaker
  • Lime Squeezer
  • Jigger
  • Mushroom Glass(preferred)
  • Muddler

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Don Julio Repasado
  • 1 oz Pineapple Juice
  • 1 Lime
  • Simple Syrup
  • Blue Curaco
  • Strawberries
  • Blueberries
  • A Bag of Ice

Directions

  1. Fill your mushroom glass with ice and 1 ounce of Blue Curaco
  2. In a Boston Shaker, grab 3 fresh strawberries and muddle with simple syrup.
  3. Add in 2 ounces of Don Julio Reposado, or your favorite tequila
  4. Add in 1 ounce of Pineapple Juice
  5. Squeeze half of a fresh lime and add a splash of lime juice
  6. Shake vigorously for 10 seconds. → Learn how to hold, seal, and open a Boston Shaker properly.
  7. Strain over ice and garnish with a sliced strawberry and blueberry

Mocktail Variation

This cocktail can become a berrylicious mocktail. Substitute Blue Curaco for blueberry syrup and leave the Don Julio on the shelf!
