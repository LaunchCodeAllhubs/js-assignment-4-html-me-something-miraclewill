Equipment Needed

  • Boston Shaker
  • Lime Squeezer
  • Jigger
  • Mushroom Glass(preferred)
  • Muddler

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Don Julio Repasado
  • 1 oz Pineapple Juice
  • 1 Lime
  • Simple Syrup
  • Blue Curaco
  • Strawberries
  • Blueberries
  • A Bag of Ice

Directions

  1. Fill your mushroom glass with ice and 1 ounce of Blue Curaco
  2. In a Boston Shaker, grab 3 fresh strawberries and muddle with simple syrup.
  3. Add in 2 ounces of Don Julio Reposado, or your favorite tequila
  4. Add in 1 ounce of Pineapple Juice
  5. Squeeze half of a fresh lime and add a splash of lime juice
  6. Shake vigorously for 10 seconds
  7. Strain over ice and garnish with a sliced strawberry and blueberry
